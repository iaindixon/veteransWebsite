Info

ARMY

One of the five uniformed divisions of the united states armed forces, founded on the 14th of June, 1775. The US Army conducts operations on land-based regions of US control and Interest. The Army has been active in nearly all military engagements, initially forming during the American War for independance as the continental army. During WW2 The US Army invaded Axis controlled regions, along with the other Allied forces. Operation Overlord was a major success of the the Army, coming at great cost. The Army fought during the Vietnam conflict, being a major portion of the US Armed forces in Vietnam.

NAVY

One of the five uniformed divisions of the united states armed forces, founded on the 13th of October, 1775. The US Navy protects the United Stateâ€™s Coasts from enemy invasion, transports supplies and personnel and protects regions of US interest. The Navy transported large quantities of soldiers to and from the United states in many Conflicts. Most Notably, the Navy Bombarded the shores of Normandy and supplied crafts for transportation and protection during operation overlord. During the Cold war, the Navy made several important contributions to United States Interest. Blockading Cuba during the Cuban Missile crisis, protecting transports and deterring nuclear escalation through patrol and possession of nuclear weapons.

AIRFORCE

One of the five uniformed division of the united states armed forces, founded on the 18th of September, 1947. The US Air force protects US and NATO Airspace, along with associated US Controlled regions. The US Air force has offered protection to other armed forces during conflicts, such as the Army and Marines. Support and transportation is another extremely important contribution that the Air Force provided, saving many lives in the quick and speedy gathering and distribution of supplies, soldiers and other assets. Notably, transporting Army and Marine Forces during the Vietnam conflict, executing airstrikes and gathering intelligence.

MARINES

One of the five uniformed divisions of the united states armed forces, founded 11th of July, 1798. The US Marine Corp is a rapid-deploy armed land force used in most of the US military engagements. The US Marine Corp has a close relationship with the US Navy, as they were their main source of quick deploy transportation. Notably used in the Pacific theater during WW2, and being extremely hardy. During Vietnam conflict the marines were a major contributor to the land-based invasions along with the US Army.

COAST GUARD

One of the five uniformed divisions of the united states armed forces, founded on the 4th of August, 1790. The US Coast Guard protect the United Stateâ€™s Maritime interests and coasts of US territories. They were used in many military engagements, predominantly to pilot assault crafts for invasions. Most notably, the Coast guard's piloted the assault crafts during the operation overlord, and the Vietnam Conflict.
